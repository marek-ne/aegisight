Risk Audit

Upload a CSV using your incident logs to generate an immediate retrospective risk assessment.

Data Format Requirements

To verify the Risk Engine's accuracy against your historical incidents, your CSV must contain the following columns:

  • timestamp - ISO 8601 format (e.g., 2025-12-15T14:30:00Z)
  • cpu_load - Float (0-100) representing CPU utilization
  • ticket_velocity - Integer count of new tickets in that hour
  • is_anomaly - 0 (Normal) or 1 (Known Issue) for ground truth verification
